Brand Brain Workbook

In Day 1 you will build your first AI teammate from scratch — live, in the room. But the session moves fast. The more you prepare now, the better your teammate performs.

Your job before the session: Complete this Brand Brain Workbook. It becomes the onboarding manual you upload directly into your Claude Project.

"Just like a real hire — the better the onboarding manual, the faster they get to work."

Knowledge Base Files — What to Upload

These are the files you'll upload directly into your Claude Project. The more context, the better the output.
This completed Brand Brain Workbook (exported as PDF or text)
The single most important file. Export before the session.
Your best-performing blog post or article (PDF or text)
Teaches Claude your writing style through example, not just description.
A sales call or client interview transcript Optional
Reveals the exact language your customers use.
Brand guidelines or style guide document Optional
Colours, fonts, visual rules — for full brand consistency.
3–5 customer testimonials or reviews Optional
Social proof language in the customer's own words.
A competitor's homepage or landing page content Optional
Helps Claude understand market positioning and differentiation.

5.1 — What is the one primary task you most want this teammate to execute for you?
