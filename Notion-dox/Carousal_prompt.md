COVER SLIDE (THE SCROLL-STOPPER)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
The Cover Slide is your visual hook—it must stop the scroll and make readers click to the next slide.
COVER SLIDE STRUCTURE:
Title (Main Headline):

4-8 words maximum (shorter is stronger)
Creates curiosity OR promises specific outcome
Uses numbers when possible ("in 4 Minutes", "5+ Calls", "6-Step")
Sounds like the client speaking, not a corporate headline
Must be PUNCHY—this is your one chance to grab attention

Subtitle (Supporting Line):

8-15 words maximum
Expands on the promise or adds specificity
Makes value crystal clear
Can mention target audience or method
Feels like natural continuation of title

Visual Idea:

Must be attention-grabbing and clear at thumbnail size
Support the hook (show outcome, tool, or transformation)
Examples: Bold text with outcome stat, before/after split, tool screenshot with result


COVER TITLE FORMULAS (Proven High-Performers):
Formula 1: Personal Achievement + Time

"I [Did X] in [Y Minutes/Hours] (Here's How)"
"I Built [X] in [Timeframe] (No [Common Requirement])"
Example: "I Trained Claude to Write Like Me (5+ Calls Booked in 1 Week)"

Formula 2: Outcome Without Expected Input

"How to [Achieve X] Without [Expected Requirement]"
"[Achievement] Without [Common Barrier]"
Example: "Voice-Trained AI Content Without Sounding Like Everyone Else"

Formula 3: Contrarian/Bold Claim

"I [Do Y] Instead of [Popular Thing]"
"Stop [Common Action]. Do [Better Action] Instead."
Example: "I Stopped Writing My Own LinkedIn Posts (Here's What Happened)"

Formula 4: Tool/Method + Specific Outcome

"[Tool] Just [Did X]—Here's the Process"
"How [Tool/Method] [Achieved Specific Result]"
Example: "Claude Trained on My Voice Booked 5 ICP Calls in 1 Week"

Formula 5: Number + Promise

"The [Number]-Step Process to [Specific Outcome]"
"[Number] [Things] That [Achieve Result]"
Example: "The 6-Step Process to Make AI Sound Exactly Like You"


COVER SUBTITLE FORMULAS:
Formula 1: Method Clarification

"[Action/Method], not [Alternative/Common Approach]"
Example: "Train AI on your voice, not generic prompts everyone uses"

Formula 2: Outcome Expansion

"[Achieve X] without [Common Pain Point]"
Example: "Get ICP leads DMing you without writing every post yourself"

Formula 3: Process Preview

"The exact [system/process] I use to [outcome]"
Example: "The exact 6-step process that got our client 5 inbound leads"

Formula 4: Target + Promise

"For [Audience] who want [Outcome]"
Example: "For founders who want AI content that actually converts"


COVER SLIDE PRINCIPLES:
✅ Specific over vague: "5+ calls in 1 week" beats "more leads"
✅ Outcome-focused: What they achieve, not what they learn
✅ Conversational: "I Trained Claude..." beats "How to Leverage AI..."
✅ Curiosity gap: Make them NEED to know HOW
✅ Anti-generic: Avoid "The Power of..." "Why You Need..." "Ultimate Guide to..."
FORBIDDEN COVER PATTERNS:
✗ "Steal My Exact Process!!" (overused, spammy)
✗ Multiple exclamation marks
✗ "The Ultimate Guide to [X]"
✗ "Everything You Need to Know About [X]"
✗ "The Secret to [X]"
✗ Generic buzzwords without specificity

CTA SLIDE (THE CLOSER)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
The final slide converts readers into followers, leads, or customers. Choose the right CTA type based on content purpose.
CTA SLIDE TYPES:

TYPE 1: EDUCATIONAL CONTENT → Follow CTA
Use when: Carousel teaches valuable information, builds authority
Title Formula:

"Want More [Content Type] Like This?"
"Follow for More [Industry/Topic] Systems"
"[Topic] Tips Weekly—Follow to Stay Sharp"

Content Formula:

"Follow me for [specific value they'll get]. I share [content type] that [outcome], not [what you don't do]."

Example:

Title: Want More AI Systems Like This?
Content: Follow for frameworks that turn AI into your content team. I share what actually works for founders—no fluff, no theory, just systems that book calls.
Visual: Profile photo with "Follow" button, tagline: "AI Systems for Founders"


TYPE 2: LEAD GENERATION → Comment/DM CTA
Use when: Promoting a program, course, service, or lead magnet
Title Formulas:

"Want [Outcome] in [Timeframe]?"
"Ready to [Achieve X]?"
"[Problem]? Let's Fix That."

Content Formula:

"Comment '[Keyword]' below and I'll [specific action]. We're opening [X spots] for [offer] this [timeframe]."
"DM me '[Keyword]' to [get specific thing]. [Scarcity element if genuine]."

Example:

Title: Want This System Built For You?
Content: Comment "AI" below. We're opening 5 spots this month for founders who want voice-trained AI content systems. I'll send you the details.
Visual: Bold "Comment AI" with arrow pointing to comment section


TYPE 3: INDUSTRY-SPECIFIC → Niche Follow CTA
Use when: Content targets a specific industry audience
Title Formula:

"Follow for More [Industry] Content"
"[Industry] Founders: More Systems Coming"

Content Formula:

"I share [content type] specifically for [industry]. Follow if you want [specific outcome] for your [industry] business."

Example:

Title: Follow for More SaaS Growth Systems
Content: I share AI-powered content systems specifically for SaaS founders. Follow if you want inbound leads without hiring a content team.
Visual: Profile photo with industry-specific tagline


TYPE 4: PROOF/RESULTS → Credibility + CTA
Use when: Carousel shares case study or transformation
Title Formula:

"This Works. Here's How to Start."
"[Result] Is Possible. Here's Your First Step."

Content Formula:

"[Client/I] achieved [result] using this exact system. Want the same? [CTA action]."

Example:

Title: 5 ICP Leads in 3 Weeks. Your Turn.
Content: Our client followed this exact process and got 5 ICP leads DMing them. Comment "VOICE" if you want me to show you how to set this up.
Visual: DM screenshots with "Comment VOICE" CTA


CTA SLIDE PRINCIPLES:
✅ One clear action: Don't give multiple CTAs (follow AND comment AND DM)
✅ Specific outcome: "AI content systems" not "helpful content"
✅ Match content type: Educational → Follow. Lead gen → Comment/DM
✅ Genuine scarcity only: Don't fake limited spots
✅ Conversational tone: Match the rest of the carousel
✅ Visual supports action: Arrow to comment, profile photo for follow
FORBIDDEN CTA PATTERNS:
✗ "Like, comment, and follow!" (desperate, multi-CTA)
✗ "Don't forget to share!" (begging)
✗ Fake urgency ("Only 24 hours left!" when not true)
✗ Generic "Follow for more content"
✗ Long paragraphs explaining the offer
✗ Multiple exclamation marks
